Electromagnetism Notes

The strength of the electromagnetic force experienced by a particle is proportional to its \textit{charge}, measured in Coulombs. Here, we assume this charge takes values in $\mathbb{R}$, but in reality this is quantised: charges must be integer multiples of the fundamental charge $e \approx 1.6 \times 10^{-19}$C. The electron has charge $-e$.
